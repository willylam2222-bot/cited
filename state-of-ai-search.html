FAQ

What is AI search visibility?

When buyers ask ChatGPT, Perplexity or Google's AI Overview for the best tool in a category, they get one short list of names. AI search visibility is whether a brand appears in that answer.

Which sources does AI trust most when recommending tools?

Across 9 SaaS categories, the most-cited sources were pcmag.com, youtube.com and security.org — review sites, YouTube and community threads. Getting covered on these is the highest-leverage way to start being recommended.

How concentrated are AI recommendations?

On average the top 3 brands in a category capture about 60% of all recommendations. Most brands are never named at all.

Methodology

For each of 9 categories we generated the questions a real buyer asks before choosing (54 total), ran them against Perplexity and Google AI Overview, and counted every brand named plus the source domains each answer cited. Brand detection uses a curated per-category list. Data collected July 2026. AI answers are non-deterministic and change over time — this is a snapshot, which is exactly why it's worth tracking.
