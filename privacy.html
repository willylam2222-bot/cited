Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-20

Cited is an AI search visibility tool. This policy explains what we collect and why.

What we collect

Only what you send us to run a report: your brand or product name, its category, a website (optional), and the email address you ask us to send the report to. We do not track you across the web or use advertising cookies.

How we use it

To generate your AI-visibility report, to email it to you, and to follow up about it. If you subscribe to weekly tracking, we re-run and email your report on a schedule.

What we don't do

We do not sell or rent your information. We do not share it with third parties except the AI search engines we query publicly (which never receive your email).
